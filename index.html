Designer and front end coder. Master of those little boxes that render on your screen. As my fingers deliberately dart around home row, I create translations from the ones and zeros that flood your computer through wifi transmissions. Ones and zeros become human viewable information and data controls. The better I translate, the less work your brain has to dedicate to further translating the colored pixels into actions. If I get to know you well enough, my keystrokes allow you to enjoy the experience, making data submissions feel like skipping a perfectly round stone across a still lake. I am a designer and front end coder, computer to human translation is my practice.
